FAQ

Everything about CALLYN.io

What is CALLYN.io?
CALLYN.io is Pakistan's first AI voice receptionist platform. It deploys Sara AI — an artificial intelligence agent that answers business phone calls 24/7 in Urdu and English. Founded in 2026 and currently live at a leading eye hospital in Punjab, Pakistan.
What does Sara AI actually do on a call?
Sara answers the phone, understands the caller's language automatically, answers questions about your services, timings and prices, books appointments or takes orders, and then sends a WhatsApp summary to you and a confirmation to the caller. She handles 4–5 calls simultaneously and never takes leave.
Which languages does CALLYN support?
Urdu and English — with a natural Pakistani accent, not an Indian Hindi voice. Sara detects the caller's language automatically, and handles the Urdu-English mix Pakistani callers actually speak, switching mid-sentence when they do.
How much does CALLYN cost?
Starter — Rs. 20,000/month (300 minutes, Urdu + English). Growth — Rs. 40,000/month (800 minutes, store integrations). Scale — Rs. 90,000/month (1,600 minutes, custom AI voice & personality). Every plan includes a 7-day free trial with no credit card. Pay via JazzCash, EasyPaisa or bank transfer.
Is CALLYN built for clinics and hospitals?
Yes — medical practices are our core focus. Sara is live at a leading eye hospital in Punjab where she achieved a 94% call answer rate in her first 7-day trial: booking appointments for multiple doctors, sharing schedules and fees in Urdu, and confirming everything on WhatsApp. Pakistan has 50,000+ registered clinics; CALLYN is the first AI receptionist built for them.
How is CALLYN different from Rosie AI, Goodcall or Uplift AI?
Rosie and Goodcall are excellent US products — but they speak English, price in dollars, and have no concept of JazzCash or a caller who opens with "AOA bhai". Uplift AI offers Urdu voice as a developer API, which means hiring engineers to build a product around it. CALLYN is the only ready-to-use AI receptionist made for Pakistani businesses: PKR pricing, local payments, natural Urdu and English, WhatsApp-first summaries, and personal onboarding in 24 hours.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
None. You tell us about your business in a 10-minute WhatsApp conversation; we configure Sara, assign your number, and you go live within 24 hours. Call forwarding from your existing number takes one dial code.
